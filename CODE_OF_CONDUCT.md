# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in the
vellum project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age,
body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics,
gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic
status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being kind, patient and respectful.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and community.

Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments,
personal or political attacks, publishing others' private information, and any
conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional
setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be
reported to the maintainer via the `freema` GitHub profile. All complaints will
be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers may remove, edit
or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently ban any contributor
for behaviours they deem inappropriate.
